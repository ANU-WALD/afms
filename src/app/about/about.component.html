Australian Flammability Monitoring System

The The Australian Flammability Monitoring System (AFMS) web explorer makes spatial information on Live Fuel Moisture Content (FMC) derived from satellite observations easier and faster to access.

The overarching objective is to assist users in better resources allocation in fire protection and response and improved awareness of fire hazards to people and property.

The AFMS allows users to visualise and interpret national-scale information on FMC and flammability as maps or graphs.

Data can be compared to preceding years or downloaded for further analysis.
