Australian Flammability Monitoring System

The Australian Flammability Monitoring System (AFMS) web explorer provides easier and faster access to spatial information on:

  • Live Fuel Moisture Content (FMC), in kg water per kg dry matter expressed as a percentage.
  • Uncertainty in the FMC values, in the same units.
  • A Flammability Index (FI), providing a relative measure of fuel flammability between 0 and 1.
  • Soil moisture content near the surface (0-10 cm), in m3 water per m3 of soil volume.
  • Soil moisture content in the shallow soil (10-35 cm), in the same units.

The overarching objective is to assist users in better resources allocation in fire protection and response and improved awareness of fire hazards to people and property.

The AFMS allows users to visualise and interpret national-scale information on FMC, flammability and soil moisture as maps or graphs.

Data can be compared to preceding years or downloaded for further analysis.
